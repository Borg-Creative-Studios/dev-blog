BCS Marketing MK 4

Borg Creative Studios Dev Team

We have now released BCS Marketing MK 4 for use!

MK 4 features improved load times and backend resources. These backend resources improve compatibility and make it easier for us to add technical updates in the future.

For Publishers

Update from MK 3

To update from MK 3, all you have to do is replace the "www" in the embed source to "source" and we got it from there.

Install MK 4

There are two ways to install the marketing bar.

Method 1: To install MK 4 on your site (After becoming an approved publisher), paste the embed code* into the source for your website wherever you want the ads to appear.

Method 2: To install MK 4 on your site (After becoming an approved publisher), paste the script link* into the source for your website wherever you want the ads to appear. After pasting the script*, you will need to paste the function command into the <body> tag of your page.

*Approved publishers will have the embed script links emailed to them.

Platform Compatibility 

Method 1: This method is the most compatible with publishing platforms. It has been tested on Blogger, but is expected to function on a premium WordPress plan (Functions like MK3 and MK3 has been tested on WordPress).

Method 2: Best for self-hosted and static pages (GitHub Pages, GitLab Pages, etc). Basically, any platform that you have full control of the source code. This is method will provide the best compatibility.
