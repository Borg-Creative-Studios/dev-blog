Fruity Release (Pineapple 2.4.0)

Borg Creative Studios Dev Team

Coming 10-31-2021.

This is a big update for Fruity!

First, we have rebranded "Fruity - Lemon" to "Fruity - Pineapple."

Second, we made some updates to the FX tabs and introduced a new effect, Monochrome.

Third, "Mock Up Mode" is now available for use!

"Mock Up Mode" is a special browser window that lacks a control bar and branding. This is designed for those moments that you don't want the browser's design interfering with your task. Whether it is making a promo for your website, or live streaming. "Mock Up Mode" is for you.

You can get any version of Fruity HERE.
