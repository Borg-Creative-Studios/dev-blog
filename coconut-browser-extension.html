Coconut Browser Extension

Borg Creative Studios Dev Team

We are excited to announce that the Coconut Browser Extension for Safari is now available in early access, and this version is FREE!

"Sounds cool, but what is Coconut?"

Coconut is a Safari browser extension for blocking cookies and unwanted content on harmful websites.

Top features:

  • Built for Apple's Safari browser.
  • Independent block list
  • Blocks cookies on websites with poor privacy practices 
  • Blocks potentially harmful or NSFW websites
  • Blocks distractions
